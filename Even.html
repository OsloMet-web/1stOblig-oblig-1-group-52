Risør VGS

Risør VGS is a small school in Risør. It does not have many students opposed to bigger towns. In my first year there was only 300-400 students, but it got a lot less the next two years, because the neighbouring town built a new school. It caused a lot of students to attend to the new school. As of today, the school is in a bad situation, where it might get shut down due to lack of attendance.
